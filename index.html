Preguntas de repaso para Daku

1. Which of the following sentences uses the Present Simple correctly?

  • a) She don't like pizza.
  • b) She likes pizza.
  • c) She like pizza.

Incorrect. The correct answer is: b) She likes pizza.

Correct!

2. Which sentence is in the Present Continuous tense?

  • a) I will go to the store later.
  • b) I am going to the store right now.
  • c) I went to the store yesterday.

Incorrect. The correct answer is: b) I am going to the store right now.

Correct!

3. Choose the correct form of the verb in the Present Perfect.

  • a) I have been to London last year.
  • b) I have gone to London last year.
  • c) I have visited London.

Incorrect. The correct answer is: c) I have visited London.

Correct!

4. Which sentence uses "will" correctly?

  • a) I will studying tomorrow.
  • b) I will study tomorrow.
  • c) I will to study tomorrow.

Incorrect. The correct answer is: b) I will study tomorrow.

Correct!

5. Which of these sentences is correct for the Future Continuous?

  • a) She will be working at 8 o'clock tomorrow.
  • b) She will working at 8 o'clock tomorrow.
  • c) She will works at 8 o'clock tomorrow.

Incorrect. The correct answer is: a) She will be working at 8 o'clock tomorrow.

Correct!

6. Choose the correct sentence for the Past Simple.

  • a) I am visited the museum yesterday.
  • b) I visited the museum yesterday.
  • c) I visiting the museum yesterday.

Incorrect. The correct answer is: b) I visited the museum yesterday.

Correct!

7. Which is the correct question for Present Perfect?

  • a) Have you been to Paris?
  • b) Did you been to Paris?
  • c) Are you been to Paris?

Incorrect. The correct answer is: a) Have you been to Paris?

Correct!

8. Choose the correct answer for "I _____ never been to Japan."

  • a) have
  • b) had
  • c) am

Incorrect. The correct answer is: a) have.

Correct!

9. Choose the correct future form: "I _____ to the concert tomorrow."

  • a) am going
  • b) will going
  • c) will go

Incorrect. The correct answer is: a) am going.

Correct!

10. Which is the correct structure for the Present Continuous?

  • a) Subject + am/are/is + verb-ing
  • b) Subject + will + verb-ing
  • c) Subject + have + verb-ed

Incorrect. The correct answer is: a) Subject + am/are/is + verb-ing.

Correct!
